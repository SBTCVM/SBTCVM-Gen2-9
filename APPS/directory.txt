
----TOOLS----

clicalc:
	clicalc: A ternary/decimal CLI calculator capable of 9-trit integer:
	addition, multiplication, division, subtraction, inversion, absolute value,
	and modulo division.

maze:
	A nifty maze engine demo. currently only has 1 level.
diffcards:
	A 2-player card-drawing game based on a 'short' and 'long' deck of 'cards'
match:
