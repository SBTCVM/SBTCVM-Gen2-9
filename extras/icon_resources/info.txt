Most of these are icons for technologies SBTCVM currently uses, or has
in development/planning stage.

You can find the current mascot logo in vmsystem/gfx


SBTNET is a planned Networking system, similar to TCP/IP,
only emulated (obviousy), and designed for Ternary machines in mind.

CerberOS is a planned Unix-like OS, primarily intended to be written
for Gen 2's succsessor, Gen 3, though kernel & other subsystems may end
up with prototypes running within Gen 2.

SSTNPL is one of SBTCVM Gen 2-9's bundled programming languages.
